Mountains will be created between 5 Random Mountains:
Mountain_Tiny (Has almost no Goods)
Mountain_Small (Has a tiny amount of Goods)
Mountain_Medium (Has a middle amount of Goods)
Mountain_Large (Has a large amount of Goods)
Mountain_ExtraLarge (Has a lot of Goods)

A Mountain can be occuppied by a Team. If occupied, Team will get the Goodies per round.
