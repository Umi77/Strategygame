Forests will be created between 5 Random Forests:
Forest_Tiny (Has almost no Goods)
Forest_Small (Has a tiny amount of Goods)
Forest_Medium (Has a middle amount of Goods)
Forest_Large (Has a large amount of Goods)
Forest_ExtraLarge (Has a lot of Goods)

A Forest can be occuppied by a Team. If occupied, Team will get Wood per round.
