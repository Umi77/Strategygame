Pasture will be created between 5 Random Pasture:
Pasture_Tiny (Has almost no Goods)
Pasture_Small (Has a tiny amount of Goods)
Pasture_Medium (Has a middle amount of Goods)
Pasture_Large (Has a large amount of Goods)
Pasture_ExtraLarge (Has a lot of Goods)

A Pasture can be occuppied by a Team. If occupied, Team will get Food per round.
